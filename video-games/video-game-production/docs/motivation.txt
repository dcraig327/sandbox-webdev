NEGATIVE THOUGHTS:
There's a limit to how important your thoughts can be, because you are not your thoughts. Your thoughts are not your personality.
Observe your surroundings and notice how much your thoughts make up your reality.

PHYSICAL SENSATIONS:
* You gain the knowledge that all sensations are neutral at their core, that will help you when you think there's a negative sensation
* You gain awareness of your minds amplification strategies, which aren't necessarily bad

NON-PHYSICAL EMOTIONS:
* turn attention towareds this emotional state, which tend to disappear immediately

start with physical sensations, then thoughts if you are working against completing your project
